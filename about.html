About Me

As a graphic designer, I started my own Pixel Ape Media company in 2008, creating promotional materials, internal training videos, and product animations for Florida companies. Currently, I want to take my experience and help a company grow with my latest training in UX Design.

In 2005 I started my career working with Caputo Creative (C2) as a video editor and motion graphics. Early in my career, I was lucky enough to win a bronze telly away with our production Introduction to Vodcasts during my time at C2. Working with clients and collaborating with team members is a highlight I enjoyed continuing in my own business.

When not working in design, I enjoy spending time with my family, painting miniatures, and a strategic round of wargames. For up to date information about me, sign up for my newsletter, or follow my social media. Feel free to contact me as I am looking to start a career in UX design today.
