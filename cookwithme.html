COOKWITHME

A web app designed to connect cooking enthusiast with chefs for an exceptional cooking experience.

Role: Product Designer

Project Brief

A web app that connects cooks at all skill levels with professional chefs for a cooking lesson or help in the kitchen.

Problem Statement

An unexperienced cook needs a way to connect with a chef to help them cook for a date night because they are new to cooking. We will know this to be true when we see that the new cook has successfully created their dish.

Research

At the beginning of the research step the most important thing to do is find and define the problem.

Where to Begin

Competitive analysis and S.W.O.T. profile is one of the first steps taken to gather the scope of the project by seeing what other companies do that work, do not work and how features can be added to COOKWITHME.

TALKTOCHEF.com Competitive Analysis

Key Objectives

TalkToChef’s goal is to help people improve their cooking and a chance to successfully experiment in the kitchen delivering access to an on call chef to give advice or help during your cooking experience.

Overall Strategy

Sign up for free, and if the user doesn’t like the advice from the chef they don’t have to pay for the call is the main sales message. Ease of use with a mobile app and web portal system, Chef’s are not employed but users that sign up as well and gain donations or tips for the services they provide.

User Flow Plans

Getting into the persona’s mind it helped generate several user flows to establish early navigation of COOKWITHME. In return the user flows also helped created situations and user journey which established questions and opportunities for COOKWITHME success.

Ideation & Prototyping

Low to high fidelity wireframes and user test results to help shape the experience of the overall app.

Initial Prototype Thoughts

The users flows and navigation flow research had created an outline for the prototype screens and best way to approach the information for each screen. Having the research ahead of time allowed me to see what the flows are going to do was to create sketches of the screens out of pen and paper.

Low Fidelity Wireframe

Taking to the roots of design and creating the first prototype on grid paper with pen. I use this technique to first plan out a design to easily step back and take a look at the overall project.

The low fidelity wireframes were designed to follow persona flows to their goal.

Mid Fidelity Wireframe

Using Adobe XD to create a cleaner look of the original design. Adjusting the spacing of the artwork and went back to personas to create additional updates. I began working with the idea of how to show a user being logged in and changing the location of the priority call icon.

High Fidelity Wireframe & Prototyping

The High Fidelity design was created in Adobe XD and tested with a link. This was a great opportunity to see how users approached the project beyond theory and had actual use.

High Fidelity Wireframe & Prototyping

With Style Guide & Testing Update

Using Adobe XD with the information from the user tests, and additional A/B test results I created a style guide for the latest iteration. One goal of the new prototype was to shorten user flows to achieve their goal with less clicks.

Design

Establishing the overall look of COOKWITHME using a style guide and updating the app for accessibility.
